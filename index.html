Correlations Discovered Between Healthcare and Poverty

There is a simple relationship Between the healthcare and poverty. As poverty goes up, the percentage of people without healthcare goes up. Hovering over the circles for each state, you can see the exact percentage of population that are in poverty and lacks healthcare for corresponding state.

Although there is a lot of variability, but there is a correlation between the poverty rate and the poverty rate. Healthcare and poverty has positive Correlation.

Texas is a clear outlier. It has the highest percentage without healthcare by far. Although, it does have a higher level of poverty than many other states,there are a number of other states that have a higher level of those in poverty, but have a lower percentage without healthcare.
